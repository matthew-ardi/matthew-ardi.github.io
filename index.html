The Great Recession

September 15, 2008 when the fall of Lehman Brothers caused a major panic on the inter-bank loan market, marking the beginning of the rising unemployment rate during the financial crisis. The great recession technically occurred between December 2007 to June 2009 but the impact was felt until May 2016 when unemployment rate fell prior to great recession. for 6 years the United States went through gradual economic recovery
