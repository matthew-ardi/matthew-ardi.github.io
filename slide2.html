United States National Unemployment Rate

Data: Unemployed Individuals with Less than a high school diploma, Age 25 years and over

COVID-19

After several state, city and county government imposed 'stay at home' quarantines to stem the spread of the virus, facing economic uncertainty many businesses laid-off their employees, resulting in the highest unemployment rate since the great recession.

Unlike the great recession, economic recovery was made faster due to the rapid development of COVID-19 vaccine and rapid distribution of the vaccine. Businesses had more optimistic outlook at the end of pandemic that resulted in more hiring and unemployment rate back to pre-pandemic level in 2022
